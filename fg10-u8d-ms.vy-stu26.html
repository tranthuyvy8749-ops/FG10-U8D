LEARN THIS! The Passive

A. Present Perfect Passive

Structure: has / have + been + past participle (V3/ed)

  • (+) Billions of photos have been uploaded.
  • (-) The technology hasn't been perfected.
  • (?) Have traditional cameras been replaced?

B. Future Passive & Modals

Structure: will / can / may + be + past participle (V3/ed)

  • (+) Our lives will be changed by these devices.
  • (-) These devices won't be sold in shops for a while.
  • (?) Will cameras be replaced?
  • (Modals) Messages can be sent. They may be taken over.
